S T R O B O S C O P E

This app won 1st place in the WVU 2013-2014 App Design challenge. The idea behind this app was simplicity. At it's most basic level it is a strobe light application with a twist: it strobes to the beat of the music. This music can come from the microphone or from the user's iTunes playlists.
